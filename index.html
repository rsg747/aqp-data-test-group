Record integrity

Each locked submission carries an integrity seal (hash). The dashboard re-computes it to confirm no record has been altered — demonstrating the immutability of a submitted record.
Proof-of-concept notes: submissions are stored append-only in the browser’s local store on this device (same origin as the grading tools). True cross-iPad aggregation is the hosted-database step of the plan. “Meets standard” = grade 4–5; “3” is standard-with-debrief; “below” = 2 / 2-variants / 1 / 1-variants / N-A. This is a demonstration that the architecture is feasible, not a certified system.
